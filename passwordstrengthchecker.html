Tips for Creating a Strong Password

A strong password should contain a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. It should also be long enough to prevent brute force attacks.

Security experts recommend using passwords that are at least 12 characters long and unique for every account you use online.

Why Password Security Is Important

Cyber attacks are becoming more common every year. Hackers often use automated programs to guess weak passwords. Once they gain access to an account, they may steal personal information or attempt to access other accounts using the same password.

Using a password strength checker helps you identify weak passwords and improve them before using them for important services like email, banking, or social media.
