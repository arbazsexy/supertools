How to Use the GST Calculator

The GST Calculator helps you calculate Goods and Services Tax quickly and accurately. You can use it either to add GST to a base amount or to remove GST from a tax-inclusive amount. This makes it useful for billing, invoicing, price verification, and tax calculations.

To use the calculator, enter the amount, choose whether you want to add or remove GST, select a GST rate, and pick the tax type. You can use common preset GST rates such as 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%, or enter a custom GST rate if needed.

Once you click the 🧾 Calculate button, the tool will instantly show the base amount, GST amount, final amount, and the tax breakdown into CGST and SGST or IGST, depending on the type of transaction you selected.

What is GST?

GST stands for Goods and Services Tax. It is an indirect tax charged on the sale of goods and services. In India, GST replaced multiple indirect taxes and created a more unified tax structure.
