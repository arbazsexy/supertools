Salary Details

How to use this salary calculator

The Salary Calculator helps you estimate your monthly salary, yearly salary, in-hand salary, and common deductions like tax, PF, and HRA. It is useful for employees, job seekers, and anyone comparing salary offers.

To use this calculator, enter your salary amount and choose whether it is monthly, yearly, or hourly. If you select hourly, you can enter working hours per week to estimate the total salary. You can also enter a custom tax percentage or enable the India Tax Slab option for an estimated slab-based tax calculation.

The calculator then shows a clear salary breakdown including basic salary, HRA, PF, other salary components, net monthly salary, and net yearly salary.

What the result includes

  • Monthly Salary: Estimated monthly pay.
  • Yearly Salary: Total annual income.
  • Tax Amount: Estimated tax deduction.
  • Basic Salary: Salary base component.
  • HRA: House Rent Allowance estimate.
  • PF: Provident Fund deduction estimate.
  • Other Components: Remaining salary portion.
  • Net Salary: In-hand income after tax.
