How to Use the Tip Calculator

The Tip Calculator helps you quickly calculate how much tip to give on a restaurant bill, service bill, or any shared expense. It can also split the bill between multiple people, making it useful for dinners, group outings, cafes, travel, and shared payments.

To use the calculator, enter the bill amount, select a tip percentage, and enter the number of people. You can use the slider for quick tip selection or type a custom tip percentage manually. The calculator will instantly show the tip amount, total bill, bill per person, tip per person, and total per person.

This tool is useful when you want to avoid manual math and get a quick, accurate breakdown before paying. It works directly in your browser and is easy to use on both desktop and mobile devices.

What This Tip Calculator Shows

  • Tip Amount: The amount added as a tip.
  • Total Bill: Bill amount plus tip.
  • Bill Per Person: Original bill divided by the number of people.
  • Tip Per Person: Tip amount divided by the number of people.
  • Total Per Person: Final amount each person should pay.
