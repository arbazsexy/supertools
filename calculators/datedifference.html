How to Use the Date Difference Calculator

This Date Difference Calculator helps you quickly find the difference between two dates in days, weeks, months, and hours. It also includes a working days calculator, countdown feature, and custom holiday support.

Select a start date and an end date, then choose whether to exclude weekends. You can also add custom holidays in YYYY-MM-DD format separated by commas. Once you click Calculate, the tool will show a detailed breakdown instantly.

What Can This Tool Calculate?

  • Total Days: The number of calendar days between the two dates.
  • Weeks: Full and approximate week difference.
  • Months: Approximate month difference.
  • Hours: Total hours between the dates.
  • Working Days: Business days with optional weekend and holiday exclusions.
  • Countdown: Number of days remaining until the selected end date.
