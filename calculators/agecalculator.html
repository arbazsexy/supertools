How This Age Calculator Works

This calculator compares your birth date with today or with any target date you choose. It accounts for real calendar differences, including leap years, different month lengths, and exact day changes.

That is why age is not just a simple year subtraction. Two people born in the same year can still have different exact ages depending on whether their birthday has already happened in the current year.

You also get extra context like your next birthday date, days remaining, total days lived, and milestone tracking, which makes the result more useful than a basic age-only calculator.
