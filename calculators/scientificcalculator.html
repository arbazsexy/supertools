How to Use the Scientific Calculator

The Scientific Calculator is a powerful online calculator designed for students, teachers, engineers, and anyone who needs more than simple addition and subtraction. It can handle trigonometric functions, inverse trigonometric functions, logarithms, natural logarithms, square roots, powers, factorials, percentages, and complex expressions with brackets.

To use the calculator, type your expression into the display or press the calculator buttons. Then click 🧾 Calculate or press Enter on your keyboard. The calculator automatically handles many common math patterns, including implicit multiplication such as 2sin(30) or 2(5+3). You can also reuse the previous result using the ANS button.

This scientific calculator uses degrees for trigonometric functions like sin, cos, and tan, which makes it especially useful for school and college calculations. It also supports inverse trigonometric functions such as asin, acos, and atan.

Supported Functions and Features

  • Basic Operations: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Trigonometry: sin, cos, tan, asin, acos, atan.
  • Logs: log and ln.
  • Advanced Math: square root, powers, square, absolute value, factorial, and percentages.
  • Constants: π and e.
  • Keyboard Support: Use your keyboard for fast input and quick calculations.
  • Bracket Support: Solve complex expressions with parentheses.
  • Answer Reuse: Insert the previous answer using ANS.
  • Instant Results: Get answers immediately in your browser.
