Why Convert JPG to PNG?

JPG and PNG are two of the most widely used image formats. JPG files are usually smaller and ideal for photographs, while PNG files provide better image quality and support transparent backgrounds. Because of this, PNG images are often used for logos, icons, graphics, and web design elements.

Converting JPG to PNG can help preserve image details, improve editing flexibility, and make images compatible with design tools or website layouts.
