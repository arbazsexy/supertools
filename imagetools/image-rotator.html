How to Rotate an Image

The Image Rotator lets you turn an image left or right directly in your browser. It is useful when a photo appears sideways, upside down, or simply needs a better orientation for websites, documents, social posts, presentations, or personal use.

To use the tool, upload your image or drag and drop it into the upload area. Once loaded, click Rotate Left or Rotate Right to turn the image by 90 degrees at a time. You can keep rotating until the preview looks exactly how you want.

After rotating the image, you can download the final version instantly. The whole process happens in your browser, so your image is not uploaded to a server, which helps keep your files private.
