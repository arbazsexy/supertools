How This Image Rotator Works

This image rotator loads the selected JPG, PNG, or WebP file directly in your browser, draws it onto a canvas, and rotates that canvas in 90-degree steps whenever you turn the image left or right. That means you can fix sideways photos, upside-down screenshots, and misaligned document images without opening a larger editing app or uploading the file to a remote service.

After each turn, the page refreshes the live preview so you can confirm the orientation before you save the result. The download action always points to the most recent version on the page, which makes the workflow simple for people who just need a clean, correctly oriented export for a website, marketplace, form, report, or social post.

Because the rotation happens locally in the browser, the process stays quick and private for everyday use. In practice, that makes this page useful as an online photo rotator, screenshot rotator, product image rotator, and general image orientation fixer for routine publishing and sharing tasks.

If the rotated image still needs smaller dimensions, lighter file size, or another format afterward, you can move into resizing, compression, or conversion as the next step. That is why this tool fits neatly into broader image-preparation workflows rather than acting as a one-off utility.
