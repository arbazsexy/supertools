How to Use the GIF Maker

The GIF Maker lets you create animated GIFs from multiple images directly in your browser. You can upload image files, reorder the frames, control the animation speed, optionally resize the output, and generate a downloadable GIF in just a few clicks.

Upload your images in the order you want them to appear. You can drag and rearrange the frames in the timeline, use the left and right controls for quick movement, or delete any frame you do not want. Then adjust the delay or FPS, choose an output width if needed, and click Create GIF.

Once your settings are ready, the tool processes the frames and generates a preview of the final animated GIF. You can then download it instantly to your device. Since everything works in the browser, your images are not uploaded for processing.

Why Use This GIF Maker?

  • Easy GIF Creation: Turn multiple images into a single animated GIF quickly.
  • Frame Controls: Drag, move, or delete frames easily.
  • Speed Control: Set frame delay or FPS.
  • Auto Resize: Reduce output width for smaller file size.
  • Free and Browser-Based: No signup or software installation required.
  • Private Processing: Files stay on your device while the GIF is created.
