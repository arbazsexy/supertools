How to Convert JPG to PNG

The JPG to PNG Converter helps you change JPG or JPEG images into PNG format directly in your browser. This is useful when you want a PNG version of a photo or graphic for design tools, websites, presentations, documents, or editing workflows.

To use the tool, upload a JPG or JPEG image from your device and click the Convert to PNG button. The tool will instantly process the image and generate a PNG version that you can preview and download immediately.

Everything happens inside your browser, which means your file is not uploaded to a remote server. This helps keep your images private and makes the conversion process quick and simple.
