How to Use the Image Compressor

The Image Compressor helps you reduce the size of image files directly in your browser. This is useful for improving website speed, reducing storage usage, sharing images faster, and optimizing pictures for email, blogs, online forms, and social media.

To get started, drag and drop your images into the upload area or use the file picker to select them manually. Then choose a compression quality setting based on the balance you want between visual quality and file size. After that, click the Compress button to process your files.

Once the compression is finished, the tool shows the original size, compressed size, and the percentage saved for each image. You also get a before-and-after preview so you can quickly compare the result and download the optimized image immediately.

Why Use This Image Compressor?

  • Bulk Compression: Compress multiple images at the same time.
  • Fast Results: Reduce image size instantly in your browser.
  • Privacy Friendly: Files stay on your device during processing.
  • Format Support: Works with JPG, PNG, and WebP uploads.

Best Uses for Image Compression

Image compression is ideal for websites, blogs, product listings, school projects, online portfolios, email attachments, and any situation where you want smaller file sizes without making images unusable. Smaller images usually load faster and can improve user experience.
