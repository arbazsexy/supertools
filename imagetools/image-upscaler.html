How This Image Upscaler Works

This image upscaler loads the selected file directly in your browser, creates a larger canvas at the target size, and redraws the image at 2x, 3x, or 4x dimensions. That gives you a larger version of the file without sending the source image to a remote server or opening a full desktop editor.

After upscaling, the page shows the original dimensions, the new dimensions, the selected scale factor, and a preview of the enlarged result. That makes it easier to judge whether the larger copy is good enough for a mockup, presentation, product draft, content preview, print test, or another simple layout need.

This workflow is best for quick size enlargement and working copies, especially when the original file is simply too small for the next step. It is important to know that increasing dimensions does not magically restore lost detail, so a blurry or low-resolution source can still look soft even when the output becomes larger.

In practice, this makes the page useful as an online image upscaler, photo enlarger, screenshot enlarger, and quick size booster for people who need more dimensions first and deeper editing later.
