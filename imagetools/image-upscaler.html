How to Upscale an Image

The Image Upscaler helps you enlarge an image by increasing its dimensions directly in your browser. This is useful when you need a bigger version of a photo for presentations, printing, websites, thumbnails, design drafts, or social media content.

To use the tool, upload your image or drag and drop it into the upload area. Then select an upscale factor such as 2x, 3x, or 4x. Click the Upscale button, and the tool will generate a larger version of your image instantly.

After processing, you can preview the upscaled image and download it right away. Since the entire process works inside your browser, your file does not get uploaded to a remote server, which helps keep your images private.
