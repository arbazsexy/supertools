How to Convert Image to WebP

The Image to WebP Converter helps you convert common image formats such as JPG and PNG into the modern WebP format directly in your browser. WebP is often used to reduce file size while keeping image quality visually strong, which makes it useful for websites, blogs, online stores, and faster-loading pages.

To use this tool, upload an image from your device or drag and drop it into the upload area. Then choose the output quality level based on whether you want better image quality or a smaller file size. Click the Convert to WebP button, and the tool will create a WebP version of your image instantly.

Once the conversion is complete, you can preview the converted image, compare the original file size with the new WebP size, and download the final WebP file. Since the processing happens inside your browser, your files stay on your device during conversion.
