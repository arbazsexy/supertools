How to Use the URL Slug Generator

The URL Slug Generator helps you convert plain text into clean, readable, and SEO-friendly slugs for blog posts, landing pages, products, and other web pages. A good slug makes your URL easier to understand for both users and search engines.

To use this tool, enter your title or text into the input box. Then choose a separator such as a hyphen or underscore. You can also choose to make the slug lowercase, remove common stop words, and limit the final slug length. Once everything is ready, click Generate Slug.

The tool removes special characters, cleans extra spaces, and creates a formatted slug instantly. You can then copy the result and use it in your website URLs, blog permalinks, or product page links.

What is a URL Slug?

A URL slug is the readable part of a URL that usually comes after the domain name. For example, in example.com/url-slug-generator, the slug is url-slug-generator.

Example: My First Blog Post! ? my-first-blog-post
