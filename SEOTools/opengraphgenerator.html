How This Open Graph Generator Works

This tool assembles the most common Open Graph fields into ready-to-copy head markup for social preview support. It creates tags for title, description, URL, image, type, site name, locale, and optional image dimensions.

These tags help platforms understand what to display when your page is shared. That means clearer titles, better descriptions, stronger preview images, and more consistent social presentation.

Open Graph tags work best when the preview title, description, image, and page content all match clearly. Consistency improves click quality and reduces confusing previews.

This is especially useful for article publishing, campaign launches, product pages, and any page that depends on social sharing visibility.

Open Graph Notes

Stronger previews come from matching good tags with good page content, clear images, and stable URLs. Choose a strong image, a readable description, and a title that matches the shared page closely.

If your preview image is weak or the URL changes often, the preview may become inconsistent or less useful. Clean social metadata works best when the page itself also delivers on the preview promise.
