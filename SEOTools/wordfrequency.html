How to Use the Word Frequency Counter

The Word Frequency Counter helps you analyze how often words appear in a block of text. It is useful for SEO, writing, editing, proofreading, and content analysis because it highlights repeated words and common terms quickly.

To use the tool, paste or type your text into the input box. Then choose whether you want to ignore common stop words, convert all words to lowercase, set a minimum word length, and decide how many results you want to display. After that, click Analyze.

The tool calculates total words, unique words, repeated words, character count, and word frequency percentages. It then shows the most repeated words in a clear table so you can better understand your text patterns.

What is Word Frequency?

Word frequency means how many times a word appears in a text. For example, if the word content appears 5 times in a 100-word article, its frequency count is 5 and its percentage is 5%.

Frequency Percentage = (Word Count ÷ Total Words) × 100
