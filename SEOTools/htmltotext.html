How This HTML to Text Converter Works

The tool parses your HTML in the browser, removes non-content elements, and extracts readable plain text from the remaining structure.

Scripts, styles, and similar non-visible elements are removed before text extraction. You can keep line breaks for readability, collapse extra spaces for cleaner output, and remove blank lines when you want tighter text.

This makes the tool useful for SEO reviews, content cleanup, migration prep, and developer or editor workflows where the visible text matters more than the markup.
