How This Keyword Density Checker Works

This tool cleans and tokenizes your content, then counts how often words and phrases appear based on your selected filters. It calculates keyword frequency and density percentages so you can quickly see which terms are repeated most often.

Stop words can be excluded to surface more meaningful terms. You can include bigrams and trigrams to see repeated phrases, and adjust minimum word length to ignore short tokens that are less useful for SEO analysis.

This makes it easier to spot overuse, repetition, and content balance issues before publishing. It is useful when reviewing blog posts, service pages, landing pages, product copy, and SEO articles that need stronger keyword control.

The goal is not to chase one exact percentage. Instead, use the output to understand keyword balance and write content that feels natural, focused, and genuinely helpful for readers.

Keyword Density Notes

Keyword density is a useful signal, but content quality and clarity matter more than a single percentage target. Use density checks to identify repetition, not to force awkward keyword stuffing into your content.

Strong SEO writing usually comes from clear structure, useful information, natural topic coverage, and readable phrasing. This tool helps you spot overuse patterns so you can improve balance, not chase one rigid number.
