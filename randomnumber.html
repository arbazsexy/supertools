Randomness and Probability Distribution

Our generator produces numbers with a Uniform Distribution. This means that every number within your specified range has an exactly equal chance of being selected. This is vital for fair drawings and statistical accuracy.

How to use for Giveaways:

  1. Assign a number to each participant (e.g., 1 to 150).
  2. Enter 1 in the Min and 150 in the Max box.
  3. Set "Amount" to the number of winners you want.
  4. Check "Unique numbers" to ensure one person doesn't win twice!
