How to Use the Case Converter

Whether you accidentally left your Caps Lock on or need to format a title for a blog post, our Online Case Converter makes the job easy. Simply paste your text into the area above and choose one of the transformation options.

Formatting Options Explained

  • Sentence Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every sentence. Great for fixing long blocks of text.
  • Capitalize Words: Often called Title Case, this is perfect for headlines and book titles.
  • Alternating Case: Converts your text into a "MoCkInG" style commonly used in social media memes.

Why Word and Character Counts Matter

Social media platforms like Twitter (280 chars) and Google Meta Descriptions (160 chars) have strict limits. Our tool serves as a live word counter, allowing you to edit and format your text simultaneously while staying within character limits.
