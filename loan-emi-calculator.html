Free Online Loan EMI Calculator

The Loan EMI Calculator helps you quickly calculate the monthly installment (EMI) you need to pay for a loan. EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment and represents the fixed amount paid every month until the loan is fully repaid.

This tool is useful when planning loans such as home loans, car loans, personal loans, or education loans. By entering the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure, you can instantly see your monthly EMI and total repayment amount.

How to Use the Loan EMI Calculator

  • Enter the total loan amount.
  • Enter the annual interest rate.
  • Enter the loan tenure in years.
  • Click the Calculate EMI button.
  • The calculator will show your monthly EMI, total interest, and total payment.

Features of Our EMI Calculator

  • Instant EMI calculation

  • Displays total repayment amount
  • Mobile-friendly and easy to use

What Is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment made by a borrower to a lender. Each EMI payment includes both principal repayment and interest charges.

Using an EMI calculator helps borrowers plan their finances better and understand their monthly financial commitments before taking a loan.
