How to Use the Random Number Generator

The Random Number Generator is a free online tool that helps you generate random numbers instantly within any range you choose. It is useful for raffles, giveaways, lucky draws, classroom activities, games, simulations, and testing workflows.

To use the tool, enter your minimum and maximum number range. Then choose how many random numbers you want to generate. If you want each result to be different, enable the Unique numbers option. After clicking the Generate Numbers button, the results will appear instantly.

You can copy the generated numbers or download them as a CSV file. This makes the tool especially useful for giveaways, lottery-style picks, classroom examples, random experiments, team assignments, and fair winner selection.
