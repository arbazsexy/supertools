How to Use the Business Name Generator

The Business Name Generator is a free online tool that helps you quickly create catchy, creative, and brandable names for your startup, company, online store, agency, or personal brand. Instead of struggling to come up with ideas manually, you can generate multiple business names instantly based on your niche, keyword, and preferred style.

To use the generator, start by entering an optional keyword. This can be your niche, product type, or a word you want in your brand name, such as tech, coffee, fashion, digital, fitness, or finance. Then choose a business category and a name style such as brandable, modern, professional, short, or premium.

You can also choose how many names you want to generate at once. After clicking the Generate Names button, the tool will instantly create a list of fresh business name ideas. You can copy individual names or copy all generated names together in one click.
