How to Use the QR Code Generator

The QR Code Generator is a free online tool that helps you create static QR codes instantly for websites, text, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS messages, WiFi sharing, and vCard contact cards. These QR codes are generated directly in your browser and can be downloaded as PNG or SVG files.

To use the tool, select the QR code type you want, fill in the required fields, and choose your preferred size, colors, and error correction level. You can also upload an optional center logo for branding. Then click Generate QR Code to preview the result.

Once your QR code is ready, you can download it, print it, or copy the encoded content. Before publishing or printing a branded QR code, scan it with a phone to confirm that it still works correctly.
