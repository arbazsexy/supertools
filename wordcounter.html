Improve Your Writing with Text Analysis

Writing is more than just putting words on a page; it’s about meeting specific goals. Whether you are aiming for a 2,000-word essay or a 160-character Meta description, our tool provides the real-time data you need to edit with precision.

Key Writing Metrics

  • Character Count: Vital for digital marketing and SEO where pixel-width and character limits determine visibility.
  • Sentence Analysis: Helps writers understand their "pacing." A high sentence count with a low word count indicates a punchy, fast-paced style.
  • Reading Time Estimator: We use an average reading speed of 225 words per minute. This helps bloggers set expectations for their audience.

SEO Best Practices for Content Length

While there is no "perfect" word count for Google, data suggests that long-form content (1,500+ words) often ranks higher for competitive keywords because it covers topics in more depth. Conversely, product pages should be concise and direct.
