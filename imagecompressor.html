Why Should You Compress Images?

In 2026, website speed is a major ranking factor for Google. High-resolution images can slow down your site, leading to a poor user experience. Using an online image compressor helps you reduce the file size of your JPEGs and PNGs, allowing your pages to load instantly on mobile devices.

100% Client-Side Privacy

Your privacy is our priority. Unlike other image optimizers, SuperTools processes your images locally in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, meaning your personal data never leaves your computer. This makes our tool one of the safest ways to shrink image sizes online.
