Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

This page explains how SuperTools handles information when you browse the site, use tools, submit a message, or interact with analytics and advertising services. We aim to keep this policy readable and practical rather than overly technical.

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Browser-based where possible

Many tools are designed so inputs and outputs can be handled directly in your browser.

Analytics and ads

We may use analytics and advertising services to understand traffic and help support the site.

Contact requests

If you contact us, we may use the information you provide to respond to your message.

1. Information we may collect

We may collect limited technical and usage information such as browser type, device type, general page activity, referring pages, and tool interactions. This helps us understand site performance, improve usability, and identify which parts of the site are most helpful.

2. Browser-based processing

Many SuperTools pages are designed for browser-based processing. For those tools, your text, files, or generated output may be handled locally in your browser as part of the core tool workflow rather than being uploaded to our servers for the primary action.

That said, individual tools can differ, so the exact behavior may depend on the page, the libraries used, and the feature itself.

3. Analytics

We may use analytics services such as Google Analytics to understand traffic, measure engagement, monitor site quality, and improve the tool experience. These services may collect anonymous or pseudonymous usage information in line with their own policies.

4. Advertising and cookies

SuperTools may use advertising services such as Google AdSense. These services can use cookies or similar technologies to measure performance, understand ad interactions, and show more relevant advertising.

  • You may be able to limit or block cookies through your browser settings.
  • Blocking some cookies can affect parts of how websites or ads behave.
  • Third-party ad providers may have their own privacy practices and controls.

5. Contact submissions

If you contact us through the site, the information you provide, such as your name, email address, and message, may be used to respond to your support request, feedback, suggestion, or bug report.

6. Third-party links and services

Some pages may link to or rely on third-party services, libraries, form handlers, analytics services, advertising providers, or external tools. Those services may operate under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them where relevant.

7. Data retention and security

We aim to limit unnecessary data handling and keep the site simple, but no website or online service can promise perfect security. If you send us information through a contact form or email, we will handle it with reasonable care in the context of operating and improving the site.

8. Children’s privacy

SuperTools is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe information has been submitted in error, please contact us and we will review the request.

9. Policy updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time as the site grows, tools change, or services are added or removed. When we make updates, the revised version will be reflected on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please visit the Contact page and send us a message.
