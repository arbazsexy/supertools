How to Merge PDF Files

The Merge PDF tool helps you combine multiple PDF files into one document in just a few clicks. It is useful when you want to join reports, assignments, invoices, forms, notes, or scanned documents into a single PDF file.

To use this tool, upload two or more PDF files using the upload box above. You can drag and drop files or click to select them. After uploading, you can reorder the files by dragging them or by using the move buttons. You will also see a small preview of the first page of each PDF.

Once the files are in the correct order, click the Merge PDFs button. The tool will combine them directly in your browser and generate a downloadable merged PDF file. This means your files stay on your device for better privacy and fast performance.
