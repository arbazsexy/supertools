Privacy Policy

SubScrubAI ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered subscription tracking and expense management application designed to help users monitor recurring subscriptions, renewal dates, and digital service expenses.

By using SubScrubAI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting user privacy and handling personal information responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

SubScrubAI may collect and process the following categories of information:

Account Information

  • Name

  • Authentication information

Subscription & Usage Data

  • Subscription names
  • Billing amounts
  • Renewal dates
  • User-entered subscription information

Device Permissions & Notification Access

With user permission, SubScrubAI may access device notifications or transaction alerts to help identify recurring subscription charges and renewal events.

Notification data is processed primarily on-device whenever possible.

Optional Email Integration

If a user chooses to connect an email account through supported integrations, SubScrubAI may analyze subscription-related emails such as receipts, renewal notices, or trial confirmations to improve subscription detection and reminders.

Users may revoke access at any time through their connected account settings.

Analytics & Diagnostics

We may collect limited technical information including:

  • Device type

This information helps improve app stability and performance.

Certain AI-powered features may use secure cloud-based processing services to analyze subscription-related information and generate insights.

We do not sell user data to advertisers or third parties.
