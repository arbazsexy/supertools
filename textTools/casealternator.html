What Is Alternating Case?

Alternating case is a writing style where letters switch one by one between lowercase and uppercase. For example, hello can become hElLo or HeLlO. This style is popular in memes, humorous writing, online jokes, playful captions, and stylized internet text.

Why Use a Case Alternator?

A case alternator helps create text that feels playful, exaggerated, sarcastic, or visually distinctive. It is a simple way to turn plain text into something more expressive for online communication, jokes, or creative formatting.
