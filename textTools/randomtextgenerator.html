How This Random Text Generator Works

This tool selects words from either the built-in default word pool or from your custom comma-separated list, then combines them into random words, sentence-style lines, or full paragraph blocks depending on the output type you choose.

If you generate words, the tool gives you a simple random sequence. If you generate sentences, it combines several random words into sentence-like output. If you choose paragraphs, it groups multiple generated sentences into larger text blocks that are more useful for layouts, previews, and longer content areas.

The extra options give you more control over the final result. You can add numbers for more variation, keep punctuation for more natural sentence flow, and capitalize sentence starts when you want cleaner, more readable output.

This makes the tool useful for designers testing content space, developers filling components with sample data, students building examples, writers experimenting with ideas, and anyone who needs quick random text without manual effort.
