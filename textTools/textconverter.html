How to Use the Case Converter

The Text Case Converter helps you quickly change text into different capitalization and formatting styles. It is useful for editing titles, social posts, metadata, article headings, code-friendly text, labels, notes, and formatted content.

Paste or type your text into the input box and click the format you want. You can convert text into UPPERCASE, lowercase, Capitalize Words, Sentence case, aLtErNaTiNg case, Inverse Case, camelCase, snake_case, or kebab-case.

The tool also gives you live stats including words, characters, lines, and sentences. After conversion, you can copy the result instantly or download it as a text file.

Formatting Options Explained

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capital letters.
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters.
  • Capitalize Words: Capitalizes the first letter of each word.
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence.
  • Alternating Case: Creates a mixed-case pattern used for playful text.
  • Inverse Case: Switches uppercase to lowercase and lowercase to uppercase.
  • camelCase: Removes spaces and capitalizes each word after the first.
  • snake_case: Converts text into lowercase words joined with underscores.
  • kebab-case: Converts text into lowercase words joined with hyphens.
