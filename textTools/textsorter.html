How to Use the Text Sorter

The Text Sorter helps you arrange text lines in a clear order instantly. It is useful for sorting names, keyword lists, tags, numbers, copied records, raw datasets, and other line-based content.

To use it, paste your text into the input box with one item per line. Then choose the sort order and enable any extra options you need, such as case-sensitive sorting, numeric sorting, duplicate removal, trimming spaces, or ignoring empty lines. Click Sort Text to generate the cleaned and sorted output.

This tool is especially helpful for students, writers, SEO users, developers, and anyone working with line-based text. It saves time and makes large text lists easier to organize.

What Does a Text Sorter Do?

A text sorter reorders text lines based on alphabetical or numeric rules. For example, it can sort words from A to Z, reverse them from Z to A, or sort numbers by their actual numeric value.

apple

orange
