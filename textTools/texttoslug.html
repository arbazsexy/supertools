How This Text to Slug Tool Works

This tool takes the text you enter and converts it into a URL-friendly slug by turning it into lowercase, replacing spaces with a clean separator, removing most special characters, collapsing repeated separators, and trimming extra separator characters from the beginning and end.

That makes the output easier to use in web addresses, CMS fields, blog links, product pages, and SEO-friendly URLs. Clean slugs are easier to read, easier to share, and usually look much better than messy raw titles in a link.

The extra options give you more control. You can remove numbers when they are not needed, enable strict cleanup when you want a simpler final slug, and choose a custom separator if your project uses a different URL pattern.

The live preview also shows both the slug by itself and the full URL version using your base path, which makes it faster to check how the final link will actually look before you copy it.
