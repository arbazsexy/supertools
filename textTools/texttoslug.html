How to Use the Text to Slug Converter

The Text to Slug Converter helps you turn normal text into a clean, readable, and SEO-friendly URL slug. It removes unwanted characters, replaces spaces with separators, and makes text ready for URLs.

To use the tool, enter your text into the input box and choose whether you want the slug to use hyphens or underscores. You can also choose to force lowercase output, remove numbers, and trim duplicate separators.

Once you click Generate Slug, the tool instantly creates a clean result that you can copy or download. This is useful for blog URLs, SEO pages, article links, category pages, and many other web use cases.

What is a Slug?

A slug is the readable part of a URL that identifies a specific page. A good slug is usually short, simple, lowercase, and separated by hyphens or underscores.
