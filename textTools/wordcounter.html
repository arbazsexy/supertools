How to Use the Word Counter

The Word Counter helps you measure text length instantly. It counts words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and also estimates how long it takes to read or speak your text.

To use the tool, simply type or paste your content into the text area. The results update automatically as you type, so you can track your progress in real time without clicking a separate calculate button.

This makes the tool useful for essays, assignments, blog posts, SEO writing, social media captions, email drafts, product descriptions, and many other writing tasks.

What Does This Tool Measure?

This tool shows several useful writing metrics:

  • Words: Total number of words in your text.
  • Characters: Total number of characters including spaces.
  • No Spaces: Character count excluding spaces.
  • Sentences: Number of sentences based on punctuation.
  • Paragraphs: Number of text blocks separated by blank lines.
  • Reading Time: Estimated reading time based on average speed.
  • Speaking Time: Estimated speaking time based on average speech speed.
