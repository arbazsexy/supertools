How to Use the Remove Line Breaks Tool

The Remove Line Breaks tool helps you turn multiline text into a single clean line instantly. It is useful for cleaning copied content, formatting data, preparing CSV-style text, fixing pasted lists, and joining broken paragraphs into one continuous line.

To use the tool, paste your text into the input box and choose how the lines should be joined. You can join lines with a space, comma, pipe, or your own custom separator. You can also remove empty lines and trim extra spaces from each line before joining.

Once you click Remove Line Breaks, the cleaned output is shown instantly. You can then copy or download the final result for quick reuse.

What Does Remove Line Breaks Mean?

Removing line breaks means taking text that appears on multiple lines and joining it into a single line. This helps when you need cleaner formatting or want to prepare text for forms, tags, data fields, or lists.
