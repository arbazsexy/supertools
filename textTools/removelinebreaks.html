How This Remove Line Breaks Tool Works

This tool reads your multiline text line by line, applies any cleanup options you choose, and then joins the remaining lines together using the separator you selected. That separator can be a space, a comma and space, a pipe, or your own custom value.

Before joining the lines, the tool can also trim extra spaces from each line and remove empty lines completely. This gives you more control over the final output and helps avoid messy spacing in the cleaned result.

That makes the tool useful for much more than simple paragraph cleanup. You can use it for copied notes, form-ready text, one-line exports, comma-separated values, tags, labels, compact content blocks, and any workflow where multiline text needs to be flattened.

Because the formatting happens directly in your browser, it is fast, private, and easy to repeat. Paste the text, choose the separator, generate the result, then copy or download it for immediate reuse.
