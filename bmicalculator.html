What is Body Mass Index (BMI)?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m², where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m² is their height in metres squared.

A high BMI can indicate high body fatness. BMI screens for weight categories that may lead to health problems, but it does not diagnose the body fatness or health of an individual.

BMI Categories for Adults

BMI Range Category
Less than 18.5Underweight
18.5 – 24.9Normal weight
25.0 – 29.9Overweight
30.0 or HigherObese
