❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which calculator should I use for a salary question?

Use Salary Calculator to break down gross salary, deductions, PF, and in-hand amount. Then use GST Calculator if you need to add taxes to a product or service.

How do I plan for a loan?

Use Loan EMI Calculator to see monthly payments and total interest. Combine with Salary Calculator to check affordability against your income.

Which calculator helps with shopping discounts?

Use Discount Calculator for sale prices, then GST Calculator for final tax-inclusive totals. Use Percentage Calculator for custom discount math.

Can I calculate age in different ways?

Age Calculator gives exact age breakdown (years, months, days) and age on a specific date. Use Date Difference Calculator for gaps between any two dates.

Which calculator works for student grades?

Use GPA Calculator for grade point average from weighted courses. Use Average Calculator for mean, median, and mode from test scores.

What's the difference between these math calculators?

Scientific Calculator handles advanced functions and algebra. Binary Calculator focuses on computer science operations. Unit Converter handles measurements and Percentage Calculator focuses on percent math.

Are all calculators free and private?

Yes! All SuperTools calculators are 100% free with no signup required. All calculations happen in your browser—nothing is stored or shared.

Can I use these calculators offline?

Most calculators work offline once loaded. Your browser caches the pages, so you can refresh without internet. No data sync is needed.
