Why Use the SuperTools QR Generator?

Many online tools trick you into creating a dynamic QR code that stops working after a 14-day "free trial." SuperTools generates Static QR Codes. This means the data is encoded directly into the image itself. Once you download it, it belongs to you forever—no tracking, no expiration, no subscriptions.

How to Create a QR Code:

  1. Enter your content: Paste a website URL (e.g., your Instagram profile, YouTube video, or business website) or type in plain text.
  2. Click Generate: Our browser-side script will instantly encode your data into a matrix barcode.
  3. Test and Download: Always scan the code with your smartphone camera to ensure it works, then click "Download High-Res PNG" to save it.
Common Use Cases: Add QR codes to business cards, restaurant menus, event flyers, real estate signs, or product packaging to connect offline customers to your online brand.
